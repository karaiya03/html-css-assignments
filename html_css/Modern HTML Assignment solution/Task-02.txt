Task 2) What are  HTML entities? List out 5 commonly used HTML entities.
Ans.
HTML entities are special codes representing reserved characters in HTML, ensuring proper rendering. Here are five commonly used HTML entities:
&lt; - Less than (<)
&gt; - Greater than (>)
&amp; - Ampersand (&)
&quot; - Double quote (")
&apos; - Single quote (')
